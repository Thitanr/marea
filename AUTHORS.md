# Authors & Credits

## Creator

**Humberto Gil Valera** — LANA Technologies (Madrid)
Creator of Marea and of KAI (Kinetic Autonomous Intelligence).

Original concepts, among others:
- **Face Control**: driving an entire application with facial gestures —
  auto-scanning focus + a personal gesture the app learns by watching the
  user, so the technology adapts to the human and never the reverse.
- **Facial neurofeedback without electrodes** ("Facial Calm"): the face as
  the sensor, calibrated against the person's own resting expression.
- The Marea philosophy: radical validation, absolute privacy, one app for
  aphasia, autism and Alzheimer's — free for everyone, forever.
